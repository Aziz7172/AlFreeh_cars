About Us

We are pleased to introduce ourselves as one of the leading car rental companies in the Sultanate of Oman. We have a wide range of vehicles to meet your needs, whether you require a car for business or leisure purposes.

Our fleet includes economy cars, luxury vehicles, SUVs, and vans, all of which are well-maintained and regularly serviced to ensure your safety and comfort. We offer competitive rates and flexible rental options to suit your budget and schedule.

At Al-Fareeh Trading and Investment Company L.L.C, we pride ourselves on our excellent customer service. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff are always available to assist you with your rental needs and provide you with any information you may require about our vehicles or services.

We also offer additional services such as airport transfers, chauffeur services, and long-term rentals. Our goal is to make your rental experience as smooth and hassle-free as possible.

Thank you for considering Al-Fareeh Trading and Investment Company L.L.C for your car rental needs. We look forward to serving you and providing you with a memorable rental experience in the beautiful Sultanate of Oman.
